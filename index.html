Common Fixes

  • Purchase not applied: Open the Shop tab and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Ads not loading: Wait a few minutes, then relaunch the app and check your connection.
  • Progress concerns: Keep iCloud enabled and avoid uninstalling before progress sync completes.
